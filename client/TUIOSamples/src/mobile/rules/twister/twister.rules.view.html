You will play this game in two teams, which will be randomly generated.

The goal of Twister is to realize as many color combinations as you can. On the right, you'll see instructions of what you have to do, for example :

  • Collect 3 red pellets ;
  • Collect 6 yellow pellets ;
  • Collect 0 green pellets ;
  • Collect 4 blue pellets.

The team that has been able to realize the maximum of combinations in a given time wins! :-)
